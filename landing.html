A Migration Partner, Not Just a Vendor

Moving to the cloud is one of the highest-risk and highest-reward changes an engineering organization can make. Most failures happen not because of technology, but because of poor planning, unclear ownership, and rushed timelines. Veltrix takes a structured three-phase approach that minimizes downtime, surfaces hidden dependencies early, and gives your team the skills to own the infrastructure long after we're gone.

Phase 1: Assessment & Planning

We spend the first two to four weeks mapping your current architecture — servers, databases, networking, and third-party dependencies. The output is a migration blueprint with risk ratings for each workload, a revised cost model, and a prioritized migration backlog. Nothing moves until you have full visibility into what the move entails.

  • Learn more

Phase 2: Migration & Deployment

We execute migrations in waves, starting with stateless services and progressively tackling stateful workloads. Blue-green deployments and database replication keep your production environment live throughout. Our engineers stay on-call during every cutover window, with a tested rollback procedure ready at each step.

  • Learn more

Phase 3: Monitoring & Optimization

After go-live, we embed observability tooling (Prometheus, Grafana, CloudWatch) and run a 90-day optimization sprint to right-size your instances and eliminate waste. Clients typically see 20–35% cost reductions compared to their initial cloud spend once workloads are properly tuned for their actual usage patterns.

Ready to Start?

Most assessment engagements can begin within two weeks of contract signing. We work with a fixed-fee model for the assessment phase so you have budget certainty before committing to the full migration. Reach out and we'll schedule a 30-minute technical call to understand your environment.
